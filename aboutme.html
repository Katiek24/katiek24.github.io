20-something wandering around

(Now I realize I'm the crazy one who allowed this to happen!) Last year I worked for a Midwest financial firm and volunteered at Prepare+Prosper as a tax preparer. Now I'm in Finland and waiting to see what happens next. So for now, I'll bide my time posting random discoveries and hopelessly wait for my residency permit to be approved.

Qualifications...?
